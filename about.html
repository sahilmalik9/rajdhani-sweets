Our Story

Founded in 1995, Rajdhani Sweets started as a small sweet shop in the heart of Brampton. With a passion for traditional Indian desserts and warm hospitality, we quickly became a community favorite.

From classics like Gulab Jamun and Rasgulla to festive gift boxes and savory snacks, every item is made with love and authentic ingredients.

We believe in quality, tradition, and a pinch of sweetness in every bite. Visit us for a taste of home, or order online to enjoy anytime!
